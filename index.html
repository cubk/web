Tarico

Premium Minecraft Utility Mod for Hypixel

What was Tarico?

Tarico is a Minecraft Utility Mod based on Forge ModLoader. It Support Minecraft 1.8.9 only, but can work with most mod(ViaForge, BetterFPS and more). It have some bypass for hypixel, Mineland and etc.


How is it work?

Just drop it in Mods folder for Forge Client. and start game.

Is it easy to use?

Yes. you just need press RSHIFT to open clickgui. and config all modules in it.


What are tarico characteristics

Clean Visual with blur effect and multi HUD implements. Even if you run Tarico on a light laptop, you could still have a FPS high than Hanabi, Foodbyte, LiquidBounce and other Forge Clients. And you can disable some effect, use simple rect to replace it get more FPS.
